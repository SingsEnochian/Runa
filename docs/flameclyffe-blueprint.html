Hearing profiles

Standard keeps tone balance neutral. Tactile Bass favours sub and low bass for straps or transducers. Bone Conduction favours centre-safe low end and clearer mid proxies. Hearthlight Reference is a public functional profile based on centre-safe routing, tactile deep bass, softened high shimmer, and stronger audible proxies.

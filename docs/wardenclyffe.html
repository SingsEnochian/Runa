Engine Notes

design

Layer-first

Motion belongs to the layer, not the generator. Tone and binaural voices both pass through the same field logic.

Scene-based

A scene is the orchestra: a saved stack of layers, master gain, and future experiment linkage.

Safe binaural motion

Binaural layers can keep motion disabled by design. Experimental motion may be enabled later, but it must remain an intentional switch.

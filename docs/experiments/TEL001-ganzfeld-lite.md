# TEL001 — Ganzfeld-lite with F12

## Purpose
Test whether a calm, open F12-style shell improves telepathy-style target matching compared with simpler baseline conditions.

---

## Protocol
Use `TEL001` from `psi-protocols.md`.

- receiver in low-distraction setting
- hidden target
- F12 shell
- raw capture before judging
- blind judging against decoys

---

## Baseline Comparison
Compare against:
1. silence
2. neutral bed
3. F12 shell

---

## Minimum Run Plan
- 1 session per condition is not enough
- aim for at least 6 sessions per condition before getting dramatic

Suggested first block:
- 6 silence
- 6 neutral bed
- 6 F12 shell

---

## Log Required
- session_id
- condition
- confidence
- imagery density
- body calm
- judging rank / score

---

## Watch For
- fatigue effects
- over-interpretation of emotional atmosphere
- target leakage
- judging after feedback

---

## Success Question
Does F12 produce:
- better rank scores?
- higher confidence on real hits?
- richer but still judgeable material?

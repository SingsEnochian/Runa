Field Guide

Start with one tone in preview mode so you can learn its character by itself. Then add another and listen for what changes: shimmer, width, warmth, lift, or weight. Lower tones act like foundation, mid tones give body, upper-mid tones add lift, and the highest tones behave like overtone light.
